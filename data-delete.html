Delete Your Data

Account & data deletion request — SportyChat

⚠️

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, your profile, communities, posts, and match history cannot be recovered.

Option 1 — Delete from the app

📱
In-App Account Deletion
Instant — available in Profile → Settings
1
Open the SportyChat app and sign in to your account.
2
Tap your Profile tab at the bottom of the screen.
3
Tap the Settings icon (top right).
4
Scroll to Privacy → tap Delete Account.
5
Confirm deletion. Your account will be anonymized within 30 days.

Option 2 — Request via email

✉️
Email Deletion Request
Processed within 30 days
1
Send an email to sportychatofficialapp@gmail.com.
2
Use subject line: "Account Deletion Request — [your email]"
3
Include your registered email address or username so we can locate your account.
4
We will confirm deletion within 30 days and send a confirmation email.

What gets deleted & what is retained

Data type Action Timeline
Name, email, username, photo Anonymized / cleared 30 days
AI embeddings & personalization Permanently deleted 7 days
Posts & match records Anonymized (author removed) 30 days
Chat messages Deleted from chat history 30 days
Server & security logs Retained for security auditing 90 days
Location data Permanently deleted 7 days

Contact us

Email: sportychatofficialapp@gmail.com
Website: sportychat.app
Response: Within 30 days of receiving your request

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe your deletion request has not been handled correctly.
